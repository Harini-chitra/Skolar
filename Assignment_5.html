My Top 5 Favorite Movies

  1. Ante Sundaraniki
    • Year Released: 2022
    • Main Actors: Nani, Nazriya Nazim
    • Description: A comedy-drama film that revolves around Sundar and Leela, who come from conservative families but want to marry each other. They devise a plan to convince their parents but things go hilariously wrong.


  2. Jawan
    • Year Released: 2023
    • Main Actors: Shah Rukh Khan, Nayanthara, Vijay Sethupathi
    • Description: An action thriller film where Shah Rukh Khan plays a dual role. The movie delves into themes of justice and societal issues, with high-octane action sequences and a gripping storyline.


  3. Rockstar
    • Year Released: 2011
    • Main Actors: Ranbir Kapoor, Nargis Fakhri
    • Description: A musical drama that follows the journey of a young man who transforms from a naive college student into a rock star. The film explores his tumultuous love story and the price of fame.


  4. Majnu
    • Year Released: 2016
    • Main Actors: Nani, Anu Emmanuel, Priya Shri
    • Description: A romantic comedy that tells the story of an assistant director who falls in love with a girl he meets on a train. As he pursues her, he realizes the complexities of love and relationships.


  5. Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani
    • Year Released: 2013
    • Main Actors: Ranbir Kapoor, Deepika Padukone, Aditya Roy Kapur, Kalki Koechlin
    • Description: A coming-of-age romantic drama that captures the journey of four friends as they navigate through love, friendship, and career ambitions. The film beautifully depicts the essence of youthful aspirations and the complexities of life.
